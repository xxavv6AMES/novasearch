Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Collect

When you use Nova Search, we collect:

  • Search queries and related search activity
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, language settings)
  • IP address and approximate location data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Usage statistics and interaction data
  • Performance and error logs

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Deliver relevant search results and improve accuracy
  • Maintain and improve our services
  • Protect against abuse and unauthorized access
  • Generate anonymous, aggregated analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Debug issues and optimize performance

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist our operations
  • Partners who help provide search functionality
  • Law enforcement when legally required
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

Your Privacy Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Delete or modify your search history
  • Opt out of personalized search results
  • Control cookie settings through your browser
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Continuous monitoring for threats
