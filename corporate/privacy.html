Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard data related to our search services.

Information We Collect

When you use our search engine, we collect:

  • Search queries you enter
  • Time and date of searches
  • Browser type and language
  • IP address

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Improve search result relevance
  • Analyze search trends
  • Enhance our services
  • Maintain site security

Popular Searches

We may display aggregated, anonymized data about popular searches. This information does not identify individual users.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights and property

Cookies

Our service uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookies.

Search Query Privacy

  • Search queries are stored in a way that is not linked to individual users.
  • We use encryption to protect search data in transit.
  • Queries are associated with temporary, rotating identifiers.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized search results.
  • Clear your search history.
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Check this page for the latest version.
